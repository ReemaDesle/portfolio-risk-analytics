advanced cleaning (to be done later and compared with previous versions for accuracy attained)-
1.Entity Recognition (NER): Right now, the AI reads sentences as a whole. You could use a library like spaCy to extract the exact company name or country being talked about. This prevents the AI from mixing up "Apple" (the fruit crop) with "Apple" (the tech stock).
2.News Decay Weights: Not all weekend news is equal. A groundbreaking headline published on Friday night impacts Monday morning much harder than a headline published on Sunday afternoon. You could add an algorithm that "decays" the weight of the sentiment based on how many hours it has been since publication.
For the yFinance Data:
3.Volume Surges (Liquidity Cleaning): Price drops don't mean much if nobody is trading. You could pull Volume data from Yahoo Finance and create a "Volume Surge" column (identifying days where trading volume was 200% higher than the 30-day average).
4.Market Baseline Subtraction (Alpha): If the entire S&P 500 crashes, your tech stocks will crash too, even if tech news was positive! To clean this, you should mathematically subtract the baseline SPY returns from your tech stock returns. This isolates the actual impact of the news on the specific stock!
